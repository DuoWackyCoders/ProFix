Outdoor AC Condenser Cleaning & Basic Check

Outdoor unit only. A thorough inside-out cleaning and basic visual check designed to help your system breathe better.

Before Cleaning

After Cleaning

What’s Included

  • Safe power shut-off
  • Top grille removal with fan secured
  • Inside-out condenser coil cleaning
  • Debris removal from unit base
  • Light fin straightening where accessible
  • Reassembly and restart check
  • Visual check of capacitor, contactor, and fan operation

Important Note

This service requires temporarily shutting down and restarting your AC system. If a component is already weak or near failure, it may stop working when the system turns back on. That is usually a sign the part was already near the end of its lifespan, not caused by the cleaning itself.

Not Included

  • Indoor coil cleaning
  • Refrigerant work
  • Electrical repairs beyond basic inspection
  • Major HVAC diagnostics or repair

Recommended Schedule

At least once per year, and ideally twice per year in Texas due to heat, dust, and pollen buildup.

  • Annual cleaning: once per year
  • Seasonal cleaning: spring + late summer
