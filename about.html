About ProFix

ProFix Handy Services was built around one simple idea: homeowners deserve reliable work, clear communication, and upfront expectations.

With 15+ years of hands-on electrical, technical operations, and EV charging experience, I bring both field knowledge and a detail-focused mindset to every job.

I’ve seen how poor workmanship can create bigger problems later. That is why my focus is simple: do the work carefully, explain what was done, and help customers feel confident it was handled properly.

This website was designed to make quoting easier. For common services, you can get a starting estimate upfront. If a job has added complexity, I’ll review the details and follow up with clear next steps.
