About ProFix

ProFix Handy Services was built around one simple idea: homeowners deserve reliable work, clear communication, and upfront expectations.

With 15+ years of hands-on electrical and technical experience, I bring both field knowledge and a detail-focused mindset to every job.

Over the years, I’ve corrected a lot of poor workmanship — and that’s exactly why I take a different approach: do the work right, explain what was done, and leave it solid.

This website was designed to give customers clarity upfront. For common jobs, you can get a starting estimate instantly. If something is more complex, I’ll review it and follow up clearly.
